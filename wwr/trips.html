Available Trips

Family-Friendly

Our Family All ages can enjoy a friendly rafting trip that combines gentle stretching with excitement. We put your comfort and security first, with knowledgeable guides guiding you through picturesque waters. It's the perfect opportunity to create enduring memories while taking in the splendor of nature as a group. Prepare yourself for splashes. Stories and smiles!

Adventure

Are you prepared to challenge yourself? For those looking for an exciting and demanding day, we offer our Adventure Rafting Trip. You can anticipate a heart-pounding experience with larger rapids and faster currents. Every exciting moment will be safely navigated by our knowledgeable guides. This is an opportunity to conquer the river, embrace the wild, and create unforgettable memories—it's more than just a trip. Prepare yourself for the experience of a lifetime!

Multi-Day Expedition

To fully experience the wilderness, set out on a multi-day expedition. These excursions combine beautiful riverbank camping under the stars with exhilarating rapids. Everything from meals to cozy camps is taken care of by our knowledgeable guides. Discover the rhythm of the river, see wildlife, and develop closer relationships. This is the best river getaway, making memories that last for days.
